Patient Z

This is not just a horror game. This is psychological survival.

In Patient Z, you are Dr. Bio, a once-brilliant scientist haunted by a fatal mistake. Years ago, a patient died because of your experiment. Now that patient has returned — not as flesh, but as a terrifying ghost.

Trapped inside your own abandoned clinic, you must survive the horror you created.

👁️ Immersive Psychological Horror Experience

Patient Z delivers an intense first-person horror survival game experience set in a dark and abandoned medical facility. Every hallway hides secrets. Every shadow could be watching you. The ghost is always near.

🧩 Solve Deadly Puzzles to Survive

To escape the clinic, you must:

  • Solve environmental puzzles
  • Reactivate power systems
  • Stop lethal gas traps before suffocation
  • Find hidden keys and unlock sealed rooms
  • Uncover disturbing notes from your past experiments

🕯️ Hide or Be Hunted

This is a true hide-and-survive horror game. You cannot fight the ghost. You must:

  • Hide inside closets
  • Crawl under desks
  • Stay silent in the dark
  • Listen carefully to footsteps

🎧 Realistic 3D Audio Horror

Experience intense 3D spatial audio: Hear whispers behind you, distant doors slamming, footsteps in the corridor, and your own heartbeat rising. Sound is your only warning.

🏥 Explore an Abandoned Clinic & Face Your Guilt

The clinic is your prison. Navigate dark hospital corridors, flickering lights, locked security doors, and secret rooms while experiencing psychological hallucinations. You are not just escaping a ghost; you are confronting your past. Every room reminds you: You caused this.

🎮 Game Features

  • First-person survival horror
  • Story-driven psychological thriller
  • Immersive puzzle gameplay
  • Hide-and-seek horror mechanics
  • Realistic 3D audio
  • Atmospheric lighting and jump scares
  • Offline horror experience
